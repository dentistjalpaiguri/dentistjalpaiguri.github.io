Is a Root Canal Treatment (RCT) painful? With modern anesthesia and advanced precision tools used by Dr. Sudip Roy in Jalpaiguri, a Root Canal Treatment is generally as comfortable as a routine filling. The procedure is actually designed to remove the source of your pain. Most patients at SMILE Dental Clinic experience immediate relief following the first sitting.
How many sittings are required for an RCT? Depending on the complexity of the infection, Dr. Sudip Roy often performs Single-Visit Root Canals using rotary endodontics. However, if the infection is severe, 2 to 3 sittings may be required to ensure the canals are thoroughly sterilized before sealing.
