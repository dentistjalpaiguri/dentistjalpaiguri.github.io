Complete Dental Treatments & Services in Jalpaiguri

Welcome to Dr. S. Roy's SMILE Dental Clinic, a trusted Dentist in Jalpaiguri providing comprehensive dental treatments since 1998. Dr. Sudip Roy has been serving patients from Jalpaiguri, Siliguri, Alipurduar, Cooch Behar, Mainaguri, Dhupguri, Malbazar, the Dooars region, North Bengal, Assam, Sikkim and neighbouring Bhutan.

With more than 28 years of clinical experience, we provide personalized treatment for patients of every age—from children requiring preventive dental care to adults seeking smile correction, cosmetic dentistry, painless root canal treatment, dental implants and complete oral rehabilitation.

Our services include Digital Smile Designing (DSD), cosmetic smile makeovers, teeth whitening, veneers, dental implants, zirconia crowns, bridges, laser gum treatment, wisdom tooth surgery, braces, clear aligners, pediatric dentistry, dentures, oral medicine and preventive dental care. Every treatment plan is customised according to the patient's oral condition, functional needs and aesthetic goals.

Whether you are suffering from tooth pain, broken teeth, bleeding gums, missing teeth, bad breath, crooked teeth, wisdom tooth problems or simply want a healthier and more confident smile, our clinic aims to provide ethical, evidence-based and patient-focused dental care using modern techniques and strict sterilization protocols.
