Dr. Sudip Roy

Dr. Sudip Roy (B.D.S.) is an Indian dental surgeon and oral implantologist based in Jalpaiguri, West Bengal. He is the founder and chief dental surgeon of Dr. S. Roy’s SMILE Dental Clinic. He is widely recognized in North Bengal for his expertise in cosmetic dentistry, digital smile designing, and painless dental implants.

Early Life and Education

Dr. Roy completed his Bachelor of Dental Surgery (B.D.S.) from the prestigious Buddha Institute of Dental Sciences & Hospital (B.I.D.S.H.), Patna, in 1997. To further his expertise, he pursued advanced training in Dental Implantology at Jadavpur University.

Career

With over 27 years of clinical experience, Dr. Roy has treated over 20,000 patients. His professional journey includes:

  • North Bengal Dental College & Hospital: Hands-on clinical training.
  • Eastern Railway Divisional Hospital, Malda: Served as a Dental Surgeon.
  • Private Practice: Established Dr. S. Roy’s SMILE Dental Clinic in Jalpaiguri, which is now a leading center for advanced dental care.

Specializations

Dr. Roy specializes in:

  • Implant Dentistry: Single to full mouth implants, MRI-Safe Zirconia Crowns, and All-on-4 planning.
  • Cosmetic Dentistry: Digital Smile Design (DSD), Veneers, and Teeth Whitening.
  • Oral Surgery: Painless extractions and minor maxillofacial procedures.
