Dr. Sudip Roy, B.D.S. is a Senior Consultant Oral & Cosmetic Dental Surgeon and Certified Dental Implantologist based in Jalpaiguri, West Bengal. He is the Founder and Chief Dental Surgeon of Dr. S. Roy’s SMILE Dental Clinic.

Education

Dr. Roy completed his Bachelor of Dental Surgery (B.D.S.) from Buddha Institute of Dental Sciences & Hospital (B.I.D.S.H.), Patna in 1997. He later pursued advanced professional training in Dental Implantology from Jadavpur University.

Professional Experience

With over 27 years of clinical excellence, Dr. Sudip Roy has successfully treated more than 20,000 patients across North Bengal. He was formerly attached to North Bengal Dental College & Hospital and served as a Dental Surgeon at Eastern Railway Divisional Hospital, Malda.
