Frequently Asked Questions About Dental Results

How soon can I chew after dental implants?

Most patients can resume soft chewing within days of the procedure. However, full chewing function on the implant site is typically restored after the osseointegration period (3-6 months), once the final permanent crown is fixed by Dr. Sudip Roy.

Will my smile makeover look natural?

Yes. By using Digital Smile Design (DSD), Dr. Roy analyzes your facial proportions to ensure that the shape, color, and translucency of your veneers or crowns perfectly match your natural teeth.

Are the results shown in the videos permanent?

Dental implants are designed to be a lifelong solution. Smile makeovers and veneers are highly durable and can last 10-15+ years with proper clinical maintenance and oral hygiene habits.
