Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

Welcome to Dr. S. Roy’s SMILE Dental Clinic ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of our patients in Jalpaiguri and North Bengal.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide safe and effective dental care, including:

  • Personal Details: Name, age, phone number, address.
  • Health History: Medical conditions, allergies, and past dental treatments.
  • Digital Records: X-rays (RVG), intraoral photos, and facial photographs for Digital Smile Design (DSD).

2. Patient Consent & Confidentiality

All personal, medical, and dental information is collected with the patient’s consent and is used strictly for diagnosis, treatment, and follow-up care.

  • Medical Records: All records are maintained confidentially and accessed only by authorized clinical staff.
  • Photographs: Clinical photographs or digital records captured for treatment planning are never shared publicly without explicit patient permission.

3. Use of Information

Your data is used only for:

  • Scheduling and confirming appointments.
  • Creating personalized treatment plans (e.g., Implants, Braces, Smile Makeovers).
  • Maintaining medical and dental records in accordance with applicable professional and ethical guidelines in India.

4. Data Protection & Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your data. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to third-party marketing agencies.

Access to patient data is limited to authorized personnel only. We review our data handling practices periodically to ensure continued compliance and patient safety.

The content on our website and social media channels is for educational purposes only. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of your dentist or other qualified health providers with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition.
