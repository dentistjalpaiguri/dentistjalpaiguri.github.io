Dr. Sudip Roy is an Indian Senior Consultant Oral & Cosmetic Dental Surgeon and Certified Dental Implantologist with over 27 years of clinical experience.

He is registered with the West Bengal Dental Council (Reg. No. 1960, Part A) and is widely recognized for his expertise in Digital Smile Designing, Smile Makeovers, and Natural-looking Aesthetic Dentistry.

Dr. Roy was formerly attached to North Bengal Dental College & Hospital and Eastern Railway Divisional Hospital, and has transformed 20,000+ smiles through ethical, patient-centric dental care.
