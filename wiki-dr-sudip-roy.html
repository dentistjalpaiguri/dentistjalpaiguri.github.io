Clinical Expertise & Services

Dr. Roy utilizes modern dental technology to provide comprehensive care. His key areas of specialization include:

1. Advanced Implantology & Prosthetics

  • Dental Implants: Single tooth, bridge, and full-mouth rehabilitation (Basal/Conventional).
  • All-on-4 / All-on-6: Fixed teeth solutions for edentulous patients.
  • Zirconia Crowns: Metal-free, MRI-safe ceramic caps (5-15 year warranty).
  • BPS Dentures: Bio-functional Prosthetic System for superior fit.

2. Endodontics (Root Canal) & Conservation

  • Painless RCT: Single-sitting root canal treatment using Rotary Endodontics.
  • Re-RCT: Correction of failed root canal treatments.
  • Laser Dentistry: For gum surgeries and infection control.

3. Cosmetic Dentistry (Smile Makeover)

  • Digital Smile Design (DSD): Computer-aided smile planning.
  • Veneers & Laminates: For gap closure and tooth reshaping.
  • Teeth Whitening: Laser bleaching for instant brightness.
  • Dental Jewellery: Cosmetic crystals for tooth sparkle.
