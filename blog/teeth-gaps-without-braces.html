üö® 4 Health Risks Caused by Untreated Gaps

1. Food Trapping & Gum Infection

Gaps allow food fibers to pack tightly against the gum line, causing chronic inflammation and potentially periodontal disease.

2. Progressive Tooth Movement

Teeth move where space is available. Nearby teeth often tilt into open spaces, leading to a collapsed or imbalanced bite.

3. Uneven Bite Pressure (TMJ Risk)

Imbalanced gaps can lead to unexplained cracks in other teeth and chronic jaw joint discomfort.

4. Bone Density Loss

Missing stimulation from properly aligned teeth can lead to localized bone shrinkage over time, complicating future treatments.
