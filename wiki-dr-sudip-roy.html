Early Life and Education

Dr. Roy completed his Bachelor of Dental Surgery (B.D.S.) from the Buddha Institute of Dental Sciences & Hospital (B.I.D.S.H.), Patna, in 1997.

He subsequently pursued advanced post-graduate training in Dental Implantology at Jadavpur University, focusing on osseointegration, prosthetic rehabilitation, and All-on-4 concepts.

Career

Dr. Roy began his professional career with a clinical attachment at North Bengal Dental College & Hospital, where he gained exposure to oral surgery cases.

He later served as a Dental Surgeon at the Eastern Railway Divisional Hospital, Malda. He currently operates a private practice in Jalpaiguri, which integrates digital dental technologies, including Digital Smile Design (DSD).

Clinical Practice

Dr. Roy's practice encompasses several specialized disciplines within dentistry:

  • Implant Dentistry: Procedures ranging from single-tooth replacement to full-mouth rehabilitation.
  • Cosmetic Dentistry: Aesthetic corrections utilizing ceramic veneers and digital smile designing protocols.
  • Endodontics: Root canal therapy and restorative treatments for the preservation of natural dentition.
  • Oral Surgery: Extractions, minor oral surgery, and laser-assisted periodontal therapy.

Patient Demographics
The clinic serves a regional catchment area across North Bengal. Patients frequently travel from surrounding towns including Dhupguri, Mainaguri, Haldibari, Rajganj, Malbazar, and border regions near Assam.
