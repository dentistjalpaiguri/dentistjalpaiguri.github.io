Early Life and Education

Dr. Roy completed his Bachelor of Dental Surgery (B.D.S.) from the Buddha Institute of Dental Sciences & Hospital (B.I.D.S.H.), Patna, in 1997.

He later pursued advanced training in Dental Implantology at Jadavpur University, focusing on fixed teeth replacement, full mouth rehabilitation and All-on-4 concepts.

Career

Dr. Roy began his career at North Bengal Dental College & Hospital, where he gained early exposure to a wide range of dental and oral surgery cases.

He subsequently served as a Dental Surgeon at the Eastern Railway Divisional Hospital, Malda, before starting his private practice at Dr. S Roy‚Äôs SMILE Dental Clinic in Jalpaiguri, which now serves patients from Dhupguri, Mainaguri, Haldibari and other parts of North Bengal and Dooars.

Specializations

His clinical expertise includes:

  • Implant Dentistry: Single tooth to full mouth implants and All-on-4 planning to replace missing teeth with fixed, natural-looking teeth for long-term function and aesthetics.
  • Cosmetic Dentistry: Smile makeovers, veneers, teeth whitening and digital smile design for closing gaps, correcting uneven teeth and improving tooth colour.
  • Root Canal & Restorative Dentistry: Painless root canal treatment (RCT), tooth-coloured fillings and crowns to save and strengthen damaged teeth.
  • Oral Surgery & Gum Care: Painless extractions, minor oral surgery and gum laser therapy for gum disease and aesthetic gum reshaping.
