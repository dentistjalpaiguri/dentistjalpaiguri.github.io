Dr. Sudip Roy is an Indian dental surgeon, cosmetic dentist, and oral implantologist based in Jalpaiguri, West Bengal. He is the founder and chief dental surgeon of Dr. S. Roy’s SMILE Dental Clinic.

Early life and education

Dr. Roy completed his Bachelor of Dental Surgery (B.D.S.) from Buddha Institute of Dental Sciences & Hospital, Patna, in 1997. He later received advanced training in dental implantology from Jadavpur University.

Career

He was formerly attached to North Bengal Dental College & Hospital and served as a Dental Surgeon at Eastern Railway Divisional Hospital, Malda. He currently practices in Jalpaiguri.

Clinical practice

  • Implant dentistry and full-mouth rehabilitation
  • Cosmetic dentistry and digital smile design
  • Restorative and preventive dental care
  • Minor oral and laser-assisted surgery

Notable work

  • Early adoption of digital smile design in North Bengal
  • Implant-supported rehabilitation cases
  • Promotion of MRI-safe dental prosthetics
