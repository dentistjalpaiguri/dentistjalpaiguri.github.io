Early Life and Education

Dr. Roy completed his Bachelor of Dental Surgery (B.D.S.) from the Buddha Institute of Dental Sciences & Hospital (B.I.D.S.H.), Patna, in 1997. He later pursued advanced training in Dental Implantology at Jadavpur University.

Career

Dr. Roy began his career at North Bengal Dental College & Hospital. He subsequently served as a Dental Surgeon at the Eastern Railway Divisional Hospital, Malda. Currently, he practices at his private clinic in Jalpaiguri, which serves patients from Dhupguri, Mainaguri, and Haldibari.

Specializations

His clinical expertise includes:

  • Implant Dentistry: Single to full mouth implants and All-on-4 planning.
  • Cosmetic Dentistry: Smile makeovers, veneers, and digital smile design.
  • Oral Surgery: Painless extractions and gum laser therapy.
